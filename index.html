About Us

Welcome to Hackerspace - Girls in Tech Club! We're an after-school program in New Delhi, India dedicated to introducing young women to the exciting world of technology. We cater towards middle-school / high-school-aged girls in particular and nudge them in the direction of STEM/tech.

Accepting young girls at any skill level (be it beginners to advanced), we help girls become a part of the initiative through hands-on projects, mentorship, and collaborative learning. This is done by running coding workshops, building projects together, inviting mentors, asking questions and a whole lot of trial and error.
